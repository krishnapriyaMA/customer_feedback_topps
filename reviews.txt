Local shop were great, product easy to fit
7 months ago
Went to Topps Tiles a few months ago to look for some LVT flooring. Staff and manager were very helpful with helping us select what we wanted and offer advice. Price was competitive and our items ordered arrived in store when they said they would. The LVT flooring was easy to lay, I'd never done it before. The instructions on the box arent that detailed but a few youtube videos and googling showed me how. This particular LVT pushes in on the long length then you use a rubber mallet to fix the shorter ends, I found out which makes it really easy to lay. Many LVT laying videos show the LVT that fits together like laminate, this is a bit different and actually is easier. I would definitely use Topps tiles again for any future purchases
Q: If you visited a store, which one did you visit?
A: LongEaton
